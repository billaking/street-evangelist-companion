## ğŸ’¡ Tips for Street Ministry

### Before You Go Out

1. **Pray** - Ask God for divine appointments
2. **Review** - Refresh your scripture memory
3. **Prepare** - Bring tracts, water, snacks
4. **Partner** - Go with at least one other person

### During Ministry

1. **Listen First** - Understand their need
2. **Share Your Testimony** - Personal stories connect
3. **Use Scripture** - The Word is powerful
4. **Be Patient** - Some seeds take time
5. **Log Encounters** - Document while fresh

### After Ministry

1. **Journal** - Reflect on what happened
2. **Pray** - Intercede for those you met
3. **Follow Up** - If contact info was exchanged
4. **Testify** - Share victories with your church

### Sample Conversation Starters

| Approach | Example |
|----------|---------|
| Direct | "Do you know Jesus as your Savior?" |
| Indirect | "What do you think happens after death?" |
| Testimonial | "Can I share what God did in my life?" |
| Helpful | "Is there anything I can pray for you about?" |
| Literature | "Would you like something encouraging to read?" |

## ğŸ”§ Troubleshooting

### Plugin Not Showing

1. Check if plugin is enabled in Settings â†’ Community Plugins
2. Try reloading Obsidian (`Ctrl/Cmd + R`)
3. Check for errors in Developer Console (`Ctrl/Cmd + Shift + I`)

### Data Not Saving

1. Check that you clicked "Submit" or "Save"
2. Verify write permissions on vault folder
3. Try the "Sync Ministry Notes" command

### Notes Not Creating

1. Ensure "Create Notes" is enabled in settings
2. Check folder paths are valid
3. Verify folders exist (they should auto-create)

### Scrolling Not Working

1. Update to latest version
2. Try on different device
3. Report issue with device details
